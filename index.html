About Us

Door step car cleaning service at an affordable cost

A clean car is a happy car, who doesn't want to drive a clean car? At DAILY CARWASH we tend to change the previous car washing concept where the car owner’s had to deal with inconvenient, untrained and unprofessional people to get their prized possession clean and protected. Daily Carwash is a ground-breaking step in car cleaning sector, which provides best car cleaning service at your doorstep at your convenience with right equipment by highly trained professionals.

Mission :
  • Best quality car cleaning on a daily basis and guaranteed customer satisfaction.
  • Convenience of our customers preference is our priority, be it the time of cleaning or payment mode or frequency. Customer is our priority
  • Eco-friendly car cleaning practice by using top quality car care products with highest customer satisfaction
Vision :

Commitment to provide our customers a Happy Car to workplace every day by providing daily car cleaning service by professionals using top quality products.

WHY CHOOSE US

Daily carwash provides quality car cleaning service at your doorstep by trained professionals everyday.

ID card Sevaks

ID cards have become a crucial part of the business world. To enhance Daily car wash’s integrity and security, All our Car Swachh sevaks come with an Identity card, so that you can identify them easily for a greater accountability.

Dress Code

Wearing uniforms conveys to consumers a higher work ethic and instils a greater sense of trust and confidence. All our professionals come with a dress code so that you can easily identify them by just looking at them.

Verified Sevaks

With us, trust and security is never an issue. We don’t compromise on the safety of your vehicle. All our Car Swachh Sevaks are Police Verified using Aadhar card. You can rest assured that they can easily be trusted.

Quality Products

We never compromise on quality. One of the great benefits of using our service is the quality of the products being used to clean. We use brands like 3M and Wurth of top of the line cleaning products clean your car.

Preferred Time Services

Time isn’t the main thing, it is the only thing. We value your time, Just let us know your start time to work, we ensure you get a happy car to have a great day. You have a change in time? Just let us know in a day advance.

Exterior/Interior Cleaning

Perfection is in the details. We care for perfection in cleaning your car. With us, we make sure your car stays clean both exterior as well interior. 26 days of exterior and 4 days of vacuum cleaning in a month keeps your car clean.

Daily Supervisors

"Be a yardstick of quality. Some people aren’t used to an environment where excellence is expected" ~ Steve Jobs .We ensure our Quality supervisors visit every car everyday to ensure that you get a happy car.

Safety & Control

Our Swachh sevaks are selected, verified and trained to make them most reliable professionals in pampering your prized possession. We track, monitor and manage every step of cleaning process by the supervisors.
